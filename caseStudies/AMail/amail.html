Mail It!
AolMail is a website used to deliver electronic mail. The original design consisted of having a lot of content on the site which became a hazard. It was really hard for the user to focus on a single thing. There was also Ads that would pop up and two search bars which made it really confusing to figure out which one to use.
Project Details
AolMail also had a lot of options on their sidebar which I thought was too many options, and reducing that and clearing it up was one of my main goals. There was a hint of color that I wanted to bring out in my design, the colors were a fuschia pink and a baby blue.
The users for this site are more on the older side. So making this site very simple and accessible was the main priority. And if the design starts attracting more traffic to the site then it can be used by anyone not matters their age. This was also a constraint on the project, just the site being a bit outdated, getting people to start using it would be a hassle.
The process of my re-design was very simple. As stated before, I wanted to re-design it to be more simple and accessible. So I reduced the number of text by adding icons to portray a simple message. And I also a to-do option that people can take notes in or to leave themselves little reminders on the go.
As we want to adapt to the growing design attributes, adding in the dark mode for this re-design was a great way to start.
The outcome of this design process helped modify the current site to make it more user-friendly. Although this idea was just designing the layout, it is a step into improving the site and help it gain attention.
